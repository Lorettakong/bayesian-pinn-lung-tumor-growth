NLSTt three-scan CT-derived nodule-volume dataset
Source: STMixer/data/registration coordinates/data.csv
Volume: ellipsoid approximation pi/6 * dx_world * dy_world * dz_world / 1000 cm^3
Important: these are CT-derived lung nodule volumes, not official tumor-volume ground truth.

Selection criteria:
- Required exactly study_year (0, 1, 2) for the same nodId.
- Required positive finite dx/dy/dz world-coordinate diameters.
- Axis range: [3.0, 80.0] mm.
- Volume range: [0.02, 20.0] cm^3 at all time points.
- Max adjacent absolute log-volume change <= log(12).
- Target sample size: 800, selected by growth-class stratification.
